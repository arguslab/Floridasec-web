Welcome!

Welcome to the home page of the 1st Florida Security Workshop.

This workshop brings together researchers and practitioners in security-related fields across Florida and the neighboring area. The goal of this workshop is to provide a regular forum for presenting research and education activities in all areas related to cyber security, as well as promoting interactions and collaborations among scholars and students and between academia and industry.
